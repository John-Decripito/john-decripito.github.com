Our Story

Since its debut in 1994, PlayStation has revolutionized the gaming industry with cutting-edge technology and unforgettable exclusive titles. From the birth of Crash Bandicoot on the PS1 to the cinematic storytelling of The Last of Us, PlayStation has continually pushed the boundaries of gaming.

With every console generation, PlayStation exclusives have set new standards, delivering iconic experiences that define gaming eras.

Evolution of PlayStation

  • 1994 - PlayStation 1: Introduced 3D gaming with classics like Metal Gear Solid and Final Fantasy VII.
  • 2000 - PlayStation 2: The best-selling console of all time, home to God of War and Shadow of the Colossus.
  • 2006 - PlayStation 3: Brought HD gaming, online play, and hits like Uncharted and The Last of Us.
  • 2013 - PlayStation 4: Dominated the generation with Bloodborne, Ghost of Tsushima, and Spider-Man.
  • 2020 - PlayStation 5: A new era of gaming with ray tracing, fast SSDs, and exclusives like Demon's Souls and Horizon Forbidden West.
